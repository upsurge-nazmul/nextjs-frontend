About upsurge

upsurge is building one of the first educational gaming platform for students to learn about entrepreneurship and financial literacy, and develop other critical modern skills. We are developing games on Unity as well as HTML5 depending on the concept. We are looking to add an experienced and creative 2D Game Artist to come on board and elevate our games!

Selected candidates day-to-day responsibilities include

  1. Collaborate with a team of fellow game designers, game artists & developers to effectively implement completed art assets into a game and facilitate seamless asset integration
  2. Create art across realistic to cartoon styles, from the starting concept to game implementation
  3. Conceptualize, design, and create stunning visual effects.
  4. Create high-quality game world (maps, environments & textures).
  5. Work on concepts for characters, environments and backgrounds.
  6. Responsible for conceptualizing and creating game interface and menus

Requirements

  1. Bachelors or Masters in Fine Arts or related field
  2. Must have passion for games and involved in game design for 3-5 years
  3. Experience working with Unity Sprites & Sprite Sheets would be helpful.
  4. Must have core art skills to create high-quality game art & illustration
  5. Must have proficiency in using the relevant suite of software products - Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects
  6. Must have the ability to craft quick concept mockups

Compensation

We will not only be giving you market (or slightly above) salary, but you will also be given equity in the company and a share of revenue generated by our platform.
